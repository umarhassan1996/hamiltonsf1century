99 wins and counting: Lewis Hamilton and the century of Grand Prix wins in F1

By Umar Hassan | 07 August 2021

No driver in the 70-plus year history of Formula One has ever won 100 Grand Prix. It’s a record that would never see the light of day until now.

Mercedes’ Lewis Hamilton is on the verge of breaking yet another record in his Formula One career by becoming the first driver to win a century of Grand Prix races. In his 15th season in Formula One, Hamilton is tied with Germany’s Michael Schumacher for the most World Championships won by a driver with seven.

After winning the 2021 British Grand Prix at Silverstone, he’s now one Grand Prix away from making more Formula One history with 99 wins to his name.

How did the Briton end up on this journey to becoming Formula One’s Grand Prix-winning centurion?
